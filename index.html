Getting Started
New Member Guide

Welcome to Freemont Atlas State Roleplay

Thank you for your interest in our Roblox emergency services roleplay community. We take pride in offering a realistic, professional experience. Please read through this guide carefully before submitting your application.

01

Join Our Roblox Group

Search "Freemont Atlas State Roleplay" on Roblox and request to join our group. Group membership is required to receive your department rank and access roleplay servers.

02

Read the Community Rules

All members are expected to follow our rules at all times. Violations are taken seriously and can result in suspension or permanent removal from the community.

03

Choose Your Department

Review the FFD, FPD, FEMS, and FCOM department pages and decide where you'd like to serve. Members are only active in one department at a time during their first 30 days.

04

Create Account & Apply

Register in the Member Portal and submit your department application. Applications are reviewed by department supervisors within 48 hours of submission.

05

Complete Cadet Training

Accepted applicants will be invited to a scheduled cadet training session. Attendance is mandatory. Trainees who pass receive their official rank assignment.

06

Log Your Duty Hours

All members are required to use the Time Clock to log duty sessions. Minimum activity requirements apply. Falsifying time records is grounds for immediate removal.

Community Rules

§ 1

All members must treat fellow community members with respect at all times. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind will result in immediate removal.

§ 2

Roleplay must be realistic and immersive at all times. Fail RP, RDM (Random Death Match), VDM (Vehicle Death Match), or breaking character without command authorization is prohibited.

§ 3

Proper radio codes and department communication procedures must be used while on duty. Casual or off-topic conversation on department radio channels is not permitted.

§ 4

Authorized uniforms and department vehicles must be used at all times while on duty. Unauthorized appearance modifications or use of non-department equipment is prohibited.

§ 5

All duty sessions must be accurately logged through the Time Clock system. Session records are monitored by department leadership for activity compliance.

§ 6

All internal conflicts and disputes must be reported to department leadership through the proper chain of command. Do not attempt to resolve disputes through in-game actions.

§ 7

Members must maintain a minimum activity level as set by their department. Extended absences require prior notice to your supervisor or may result in rank reduction.
